How to Enter Subject Details:

Use the table below to enter the number of subjects you have for each credit and grade combination. Each row represents a credit value (like 1, 2, 3, 4, etc.), and each column is a grade (S, A, B, etc.). For example, if you had two 4-credit subjects with an A grade, enter "2" in the cell where the 4-credit row and A grade column intersect. After entering all your subject details, click on the "Calculate CGPA" button to view your CGPA.
